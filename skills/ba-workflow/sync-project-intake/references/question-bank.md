# Question Bank

Pick only what the input does not answer. Not a checklist. **Aim for 5-10 questions max (never >15).** Prioritize: ambiguous modules first, then missing role definitions, then business rules.

## Modules & Scope

- What does "[Module]" include - what can a user do in it?
- Is "[Module]" one screen or multiple screens?
- Is "[feature]" in scope for v1 or a future phase?
- "[X]" and "[Y]" appear separately - one module or two?
- Are there modules discussed verbally that are not in the brief/proposal?
- Does [module] support bulk actions / search / pagination?

## User Roles

- How many distinct user roles does the system have?
- What can [Role A] do that [Role B] cannot?
- Can a user have more than one role?
- Who creates user accounts - client admin or Syntactics during onboarding?
- Are roles fixed or customizable per client?

## Business Rules

- What triggers [notification / status change / action]?
- What are the valid status transitions for [entity]?
- Can a [status] be reversed once set?
- What happens to related records when [entity] is deleted?
- Are there approval workflows - who approves what?
- Are there time-based rules (expiry, auto-archive, scheduled triggers)?
- What data is required vs. optional when creating [entity]?
- How is [field] calculated?

## Data & Integrations

- Does this integrate with any existing system?
- What data is being migrated from the old system?
- Import/export needed? What format - CSV, PDF, Excel?
- Any third-party services - payment, SMS, email, maps?
- Where is data hosted - client server, cloud, Syntactics-managed?

## Technical

- Any preferred tech stack from the client?
- Browser or device requirements - mobile, tablet, specific OS?
- Performance targets - concurrent users, load?
- Compliance requirements - GDPR, HIPAA, government standards?
- Existing codebase this connects to or replaces?

## Timeline

- Hard go-live deadline?
- Phased delivery or single release?
- Who signs off on design? Who signs off on UAT?
- Has the client approved the proposal or is it still draft?

## Ambiguous or Inferred Modules

- The input implies [module] but does not describe it - is this in scope?
- I inferred [feature] from [context] - confirm in scope or mark out of scope.
- "[Term]" - does this mean [interpretation A] or [interpretation B]?
