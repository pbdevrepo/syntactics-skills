# Scoring Guide - sync-deal-qualify

Apply the highest-severity match for each dimension. Red beats Yellow beats Green.

---

## Budget

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| Green | Client stated a specific budget range, named a similar past project's cost, or confirmed budget has been approved internally |
| Yellow | Client said "we have budget" without specifics, referenced a range that sounds feasible but is unconfirmed, or noted budget approval is pending |
| Red | No budget signal at all, "just exploring options" language, public sector procurement not initiated, or rep genuinely does not know |

---

## Decision-maker

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| Green | Rep is speaking directly with the contract signer, or that person is confirmed attending the discovery call |
| Yellow | Rep is speaking with a strong internal champion who needs one level of sign-off above them; path to decision-maker is clear and short |
| Red | Rep is speaking with a junior contact or committee member with no clear path to the decision-maker; decision-maker is unknown; engagement is procurement-only with no executive or technical contact |

---

## Timeline

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| Green | Client has a hard external deadline - product launch, regulatory date, board presentation, or named quarter they are committed to |
| Yellow | Client has a target date ("ideally before Q3") but acknowledged no hard deadline; there is preference but no urgency |
| Red | No timeline mentioned, "whenever it's ready" language, or client is clearly in exploration mode with no delivery pressure |

---

## Competition

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| Green | Sole vendor - client reached out directly or was referred and confirmed they are not evaluating others |
| Yellow | Client is talking to 1-2 other vendors but the relationship is warm and no formal RFP process has started |
| Red | Formal RFP process with 5+ vendors, pure price-shopping with no preference signal toward Syntactics, or no existing relationship with the decision-maker |

---

## Problem clarity

| Score | Signal |
|---|---|
| Green | Client has a written brief, RFP, or specific feature list; they can state the problem in one sentence without prompting |
| Yellow | Client has a general idea of what they want but has not committed it to writing; the conversation surfaced a real and specific pain point even if the solution is undefined |
| Red | Client has no defined problem; they are exploring what is possible; no specific pain point surfaced in the conversation |

---

## Overall Score Logic

| Overall | Condition |
|---|---|
| Green - proceed | 4-5 dimensions Green; 0 Red |
| Yellow - proceed with flags | Mix of Green and Yellow; 0-1 Red and that Red is not Budget |
| Red - hold | Budget is Red; OR 3 or more dimensions are Red |

---

## Override Note

The rep can choose to proceed despite a Red recommendation. State the specific risk clearly and note the override. Do not soften the risk to make the override easier.
