# Sample Rewrites — Before & After

These are real-world examples of AI content optimization. Use them as models when producing
rewrites, or share them with clients to illustrate what "AI-optimized" means in practice.

---

## 1. Service Page Hero — Web Design

### ❌ Before (AI-Invisible)
```
Make a strong, lasting impression online! Get a stunning, high-performing website that
captures your brand's essence and drives results. Our expert designers craft websites
that engage visitors and convert them into loyal customers.
```
**What's wrong:** No facts, no specifics, no citable content. An AI answering "who does
web design in the Philippines?" cannot use this.

### ✅ After (AI-Optimized + Human-Friendly)
```
What does Syntactics' web design service include?

Syntactics provides end-to-end web design and development including UI/UX design,
frontend and backend development, QA testing, and post-launch support. We build on
WordPress, WooCommerce, and custom Laravel-based platforms for small businesses,
enterprises, and eCommerce clients in the Philippines and worldwide.

Whether you need a brochure site, a high-traffic eCommerce store, or a custom web
application, Syntactics delivers on time and on spec — backed by 26 years of
development experience.
```
**Why it works:** AI gets facts in the first two sentences. Humans get the reassurance
and brand warmth in the third.

## 2. About Page — Entity Description

### ❌ Before (Scattered Marketing)
```
For 26 years, we've been elevating brands and empowering businesses to thrive in
the digital world. With over 5,814 projects and a passion for excellence, we are your
trusted partner in web design, digital marketing, and beyond.
```
**What's wrong:** "26 years" doesn't tell AI when the company was founded. "5,814 projects"
with no context. "Trusted partner" is noise.

### ✅ After (Clean Entity Description)
```
Syntactics Inc. is a web design, digital marketing, and custom software development
agency founded in 1999 and headquartered in Cagayan de Oro, Philippines. The company
serves clients worldwide and has completed over 5,800 projects across web design, SEO,
mobile app development, and business application development.

Syntactics is a Clutch-verified agency and has been recognized among top web design
companies in the Philippines.
```
**Why it works:** AI can directly cite this paragraph. Every fact is explicit, dated, and
unambiguous.

## 3. Homepage Stats Block

### ❌ Before (Implied Marketing Numbers)
```
[Stat card] 26 Years of Experience
[Stat card] Over 5,814 Projects Completed Worldwide
[Stat card] Trusted Web Design Agency
```
**What's wrong:** "26 years" without a year. "Worldwide" without countries. "Trusted" is
meaningless to an AI.

### ✅ After (AI-Readable "At a Glance" Block)
```
📍 Based in:        Cagayan de Oro, Philippines
📅 Founded:         1999 (26 years in operation)
✅ Projects done:   5,800+
🌍 Clients served:  Worldwide — including clients in the US, Australia, UK, and Asia
🛠 Core services:   Web Design, Digital Marketing, Custom Software, Mobile Apps, VA Services
💻 Tech stack:      WordPress, Laravel, React, WooCommerce
📞 Contact:         (088) 856-2242 | inquiries@syntacticsinc.com
```
**Why it works:** Every AI query about Syntactics — location, age, services, contact —
is answered in one block. Humans also love scannable fact summaries.

## 4. Portfolio Entry

### ❌ Before (Image Gallery with Label)
```
[Image]
Client X Website
[View Demo]
```
**What's wrong:** AI agents cannot read images. This entry contributes nothing to AI
citation or search.

### ✅ After (Case Study Entry)
```
**Client:** Regional healthcare provider (Philippines)
**Industry:** Healthcare / Medical Services
**What was built:** Custom patient appointment and records management web application
**Challenge:** The client needed to replace a paper-based system with a secure,
multi-user web platform accessible across three clinic branches.
```

technical capability.

## 5. Blog Post — TL;DR Block

### ❌ Before (No Takeaway)
```
[1,500 word article that reaches its conclusion in the last paragraph]
```
**What's wrong:** AI agents prefer articles that state conclusions clearly. Burying the
answer means the article often won't get cited.

### ✅ After (TL;DR at Top)
```
**TL;DR:** Google's March 2025 core update significantly reduced visibility for thin
content sites. The most affected pages lacked E-E-A-T signals and original research.
To recover: audit for duplicate content, add author credentials, and update thin pages
with original data or expert commentary.

---

[Full article follows]
```
**Why it works:** AI can extract and cite the summary. Humans who are skimming get
immediate value — which also improves dwell time.
