# Question Bank — Client Discovery

Pool of discovery questions for clients with no brief or clear direction.
Ask all questions in each group — adapt wording naturally to the conversation.

---

## Business Goals

- D-BG1: What problem are you trying to solve — what is happening now that is not working?
- D-BG2: Who are your customers or end users, and what do they need from this system?
- D-BG3: What does success look like 6 months after launch?
- D-BG4: Are there competitors or existing tools you admire — what do you like about them?
- D-BG5: What would happen if this project did not get built?

## Project Type & Features

- D-PT1: Is this a website, web application, mobile app, or something else?
- D-PT2: Will users need to log in, or is this public-facing?
- D-PT3: What are the 3 most important things this system must do?
- D-PT4: Are there any existing systems this needs to connect to or replace?
- D-PT5: Do you need admin tools to manage content, users, or data?
- D-PT6: Is e-commerce or payment processing involved?

## Budget & Timeline

- D-BT1: Do you have a target launch date or a hard deadline?
- D-BT2: Is there a defined budget range for this project?
- D-BT3: Would you prefer to launch everything at once, or in phases?
- D-BT4: Have you worked with a development team before — what went well or poorly?

## Tech & Design Preferences

- D-TD1: Do you have an existing brand — logo, colors, fonts, or design guidelines?
- D-TD2: Are there websites or apps whose design you would like us to take inspiration from?
- D-TD3: Do you have any technology preferences or requirements (e.g., must use WordPress, must run on AWS)?
- D-TD4: Will you or your team maintain the system after launch, or do you need ongoing support?
- D-TD5: Do you have any existing hosting, domain, or infrastructure we should know about?
